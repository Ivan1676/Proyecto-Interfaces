Welcome to the Zombie game

The rules of the game are quite simple, you just have to use the inputs to tell de characterer whatever you want her to do.

Idle: Is the default movement of the zombie.

Attack: The character will swap his animation in to an attack movement.

Die: The character will suddenly die.

Walk: The character will show you a new mobement animation as if she is walking.
